Terpenes

Terpenes are natural aromatic molecules that create the distinctive scents found in many plants, including cannabis. The distinctive smell of citrus, for example, is produced by limonene which is also found in certain cannabis plants. Not only do terpenes produce specific aromas, they also have therapeutic effects.

Our products combine specific cannabinoids with natural plant-derived terpenes from peppermint, cinnamon, oranges and more. Peppermint for example contains the terpenes menthol, known for its uplifting and soothing effects which in combination with 5 MG of THC in Breez Original Mints creates a refreshing experience perfect for regular daytime use.

Cannabinoids: THC, CBD, CBN

The cannabis plant produces over 100 different compounds known as ‘cannabinoids’ that each possess different effects and benefits. THC and CBD are the best known and most researched of all the cannabinoids but they’re just the start. We’re learning more every day about the benefits of other powerful cannabinoids like CBN which you’ll find in our Berry Nighttime formula.
